Research

My work focuses on how human and natural drivers shape the chemistry and radiative balance of the stratosphere. I combine climate model simulations, satellite observations, and statistical methods to attribute observed variability to specific mechanisms.

Stratospheric aerosols

Limited long-term photolysis of stratospheric organic aerosols
